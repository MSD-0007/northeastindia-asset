About Assam

Assam, located in Northeast India, is renowned for its tea plantations, majestic Brahmaputra River, and diverse wildlife. The state is home to Kaziranga National Park, famous for the one-horned rhinoceros. Assam's vibrant festivals, ancient temples, and lush landscapes make it a must-visit destination. and the Divine Shaktipeeth of Maa Kamakhya located in Nilachal Hill.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Assam is from October to April when the weather is pleasant and ideal for exploring Tea Gardens, Wildlife, Historical sites and enjoying the Festivals.

Local Cuisine

Assamese cuisine includes delicacies like fish Tenga, Pithas, Bamboo shoot curries, and its world-famous Assam tea.
