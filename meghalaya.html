About Meghalaya

Meghalaya is a state in northeast India. Meghalaya was formed on 21 January 1972 by carving out two districts from the state of Assam: the United Khasi Hills and Jaintia Hills and the Garo Hills. The estimated population of Meghalaya in 2014 was 3,211,474

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Shillong is from March to June when the weather remains pleasant and cool. The surroundings are lush green, the air is fresh, and the sky is clear. It's also the perfect time for adventure activities like trekking, mountain biking, and water rappelling.

Local Cuisine

Meghalaya cuisine is known for its use of a wide variety of meat, including chicken, pork, fish, and wild game such as deer, wild boar and small animals. Some popular Meghalaya dishes include Jadoh (A traditional dish made of rice and pork), Doh-neiiong (smoked pork with bamboo shoots), and traditional stews.
